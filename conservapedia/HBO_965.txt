HBO , an abbreviation of  H ome  B ox  O ffice, is a non-standard  cable  television network. It was founded in 1972 by Charles Dolan, who is also the founder of  Cablevision . As a dedicated provider, not intermixed with  broadcast  television, HBO faces comparably low restrictions on content,  obscenity ,  profanity , and other  decency  regulations, and uses its  freedom  to create television shows with a more  film noir  feel to them. HBO is known more than any other TV network for its extreme use of  profanity .
