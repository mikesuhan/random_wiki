Oasis  was a popular alternative  rock  band hailing from  Manchester ,  England . The only constant members of the band were the brothers Liam Gallagher (vocals) and Noel Gallagher ( guitar /vocals and main songwriter).

The band broke up in 2009.
Oasis' song "All Around The World" has been featured in  commercials  for AT&T. They have had many hit singles including "Wonderwall" and "Champagne Supernova."

Oasis have seven studio albums. All of them have topped the Album Chart in the UK.
