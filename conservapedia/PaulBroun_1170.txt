Paul Collins Broun, Jr. , M.D., (born May 14, 1946)  is an outspoken social  conservative  Republican congressman, representing the 10th district of  Georgia .

He is a leading candidate for the open  U.S. Senate  seat being vacated by  Saxby Chambliss  in 2014, in one of the races most-watched by the  liberal media . [1]  He is a  Young Earth Creationist .
