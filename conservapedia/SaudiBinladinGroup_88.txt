The  Saudi Binladin Group  is a construction conglomerate, which is headquartered in  Saudi-Arabia . It was founded by Muhammad bin Laden, the father of  Osama bin Laden , in 1931. Muhammad bin Ladin was a friend of  Abd al-Aziz Ibn Saud , the king of Saudi-Arabia, who give him orders. After the death of Muhammad his son Salim bin Laden forward the Saudi Binladin Group. Today its Bakr bin Laden. The SBG group built the  Abraj Al Bait Towers  and want to construct the  Kingdom Tower .
