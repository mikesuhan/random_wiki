Owl City  is an electronica music project created by Adam Young. His hit, Fireflies, gained him popularity within the mainstream media. His songs, have a somewhat vague, but distinctly Christian leaning, especially in the song Galaxies. Unlike other artists, of the same genre, like Gary Jules, Owl city focuses on more real themes. [1]  In 2011, his third album,  All Things Bright and Beautiful , was released.

His first major song, Fireflies, became the 20th most downloaded, and the 13th most pirated song within the  United Kingdom  and  Canada .
