The  Arab League , officially the  League of Arab States , is an organization of  Arab  states, established in 1945. Originally having 7 members, the League now has a membership of 22 (including  Palestine ). During the  Syrian Civil War  the Arab League supported the opposition and blamed  Assad  for attacks. [1]

Eritrea ,  Venezuela  and  India  have observer status.
