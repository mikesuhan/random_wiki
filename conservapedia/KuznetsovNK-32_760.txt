The  Kuznetsov NK-32  is a  Soviet -designed military aircraft engine.  It is the most powerful engine ever fitted to a combat aircraft with a specific thrust of over 220 Kilo Newtons at full afterburner.  This engine was used to power the Mach 2+ capable  Tupolev Tu-160  (NATO reporting name: Blackjack) supersonic bomber and the  Tupolev Tu-144  Mach 2+ Supersonic passenger jet.
