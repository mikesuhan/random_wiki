The  Baltimore Orioles  are a  Major League Baseball  team in the  American League  Eastern Division. The Baltimore Orioles are based in  Baltimore ,  Maryland .

The Orioles were founded in 1893, as the  Milwaukee ,  Wisconsin  franchise in the minor Western League (later the American League).

World Series Titles:  3 (1966, 1970, 1983)

American League Pennants:  7 (1944, 1966, 1969, 1970, 1971, 1979, 1983)
