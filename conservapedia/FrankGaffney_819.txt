Frank Gaffney  held several defense and security positions under President  Ronald Reagan  having previously served as an aide to Senator  Henry M. Jackson . In 1988 Gaffney founded the  Center for Security Policy  in Washington, D.C., and currently holds the position of president. His recent work centers on exposing the threat of radical Islam. He cowrote  Shariah: The Threat to America.  He routinely publishes articles for many conservative journals and is a frequent guest on conservative radio and television. [1]
