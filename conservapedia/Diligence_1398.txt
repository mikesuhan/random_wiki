Diligence  is the  virtue  opposed to  sloth . [1] 
"Diligence is one of the seven capital virtues. The others are  humility ,  liberality ,  brotherly love ,  meekness ,  chastity , and  temperance . They are called capital because all the virtues we strive to practice are said to flow from these seven capital virtues." [2]

It means a careful and persistent work or effort.
