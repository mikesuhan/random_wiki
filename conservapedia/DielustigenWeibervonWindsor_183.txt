Die lustigen Weiber von Windsor  (English:  The Merry Wives of Windsor ) is an  opera  in three acts by  Carl Otto Nicolai  to a  German  libretto by  Salomon Hermann von Mosenthal . It is based on the play  The Merry Wives of Windsor  by  William Shakespeare  and its first performance was in 1849 at the Royal  Opera House  in  Berlin .
