John Hannah  is a former  National Football League  star  guard  for the  New England Patriots . He was born on April 4, 1951 in Canton,  Georgia . In his NFL career he was named an All-Pro player ten straight years from 1976 to 1985 and was voted to the  Pro Bowl  nine times. [1]  He played with the Patriots from 1973 through 1985 and played in 169 games (all starts). [2]  As one of the best players ever at his position, he was inducted into the  National Football League Hall of Fame  in 1991. [1]
