Wishaw , known as  Wishae  in  Scots , is a postindustrial town in  Lanarkshire ,  Scotland , developed on grid-pattern lines in the 19th century to service local iron works and coal mines. In 1996 an outbreak of food-poisoning in Lanarkshire which killed twenty people was traced to a butcher's shop in Wishaw. The town is represented in the Scottish parliament by  Jack McConnell .

The town's most famous sons are Tom and Jack Alexander who form the popular singing duo  The Alexander Brothers .

John Higgins, the 2007 world  snooker  champion is also from Wishaw.
