V  is the 22nd letter of the  Latin  alphabet.

In  science , an uppercase "V" is the abbreviation for the  volt .  As a variable, a lowercase "v" often represents  velocity .

In  Roman numerals , V is the  symbol  for the  number  five.

V  is also the title of two multi-episode  television  projects involving a visit to  Earth  by an  extraterrestrial  expeditionary force bent on conquest by force and deception.
