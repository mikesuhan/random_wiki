William T. "Bill" Bolling  (born June 15, 1957) is a  Republican Party  politician and a former  Lieutenant Governor  of  Virginia , having been elected in 2005 and reelected in 2009.

Bolling's first elected office was in 1991 when he served on the Hanover County Board of Supervisors. He was elected to the Senate of Virginia in 1995 and served as Chairman of the Joint Republican Caucus and the Virginia Republican Senatorial Committee.
