Hines Ward  is a  National Football League  wide receiver for the  Pittsburgh Steelers . After playing football collegiately at the  University of Georgia , he was drafted in the second round of the  1998 NFL Draft  by the  Pittsburgh Steelers . He is the Steelers all-time leader in receptions and has caught as of the beginning of the 2008 season, 65 touchdowns. He was named the MVP of  Super Bowl XL  when he caught five passes for 123 yards and a touchdown. [1]

In September, 2010, Ward was appointed to the President's Advisory Commission on Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders. [2]
