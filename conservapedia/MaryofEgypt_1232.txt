Mary of Egypt  (344 to 421 A.D.) Little is known for certain about her life.  She led an immoral existence in  Alexandria  until her conversion to  Christianity  outside the  Holy Sepulchre  in  Jerusalem  in 380.  After that she lived a solitary existence east of  Jordan  until her death 41 years later.

She was the subject of many popular legends.
