Larry Klayman  is the founder of  Judicial Watch  and Freedom Watch. An attorney who worked in the Reagan Justice Department, he has long been a conservative and judicial activist. In 2004 he made an unsuccessful run for the Republican nomination for the Senate from  Florida , but was defeated in the primary by  Mel Martinez , despite the support of  Operation Rescue  founder  Randall Terry . [1] [2]

Klayman advocates a preemptive nuclear strike on Iran. [3]  He has also written to the General Counsel of the Democratic National Committee, warning them that they must not certify their candidate  Barack Hussein Obama  eligible to stand for election to the Presidency. [4]
