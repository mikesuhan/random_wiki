Gordon D. Fee  is an American  theologian . Growing up in a  Pentecostal  tradition, he obtained his Ph.D at  University of Southern California  and taught at several institutions of higher learning in the United States and later at  Regent College  in Vancouver,  Canada . Fee is a renowned scholar on  New Testament  interpretation. He is also an ordained minister in the  Assemblies of God .

Fee supports increasing the role of women in ecclesiastical functions. [1]
