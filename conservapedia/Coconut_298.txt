A  Coconut  is a  fruit  grown on  coconut trees , largely grown in hot,  tropical  areas.  It has a thick shell.  A falling coconut can often cause serious injury to a person's head, back, and shoulders, and even death. [1] 
Coconuts can also be used to make  musical instruments  and occasionally,  clothing . The coconut volume is mostly 'milk,' a drinkable fluid composed mostly of water. [2]  Coconut juice, milk, and cream are used in  Thai  cuisine and in beverages.
