Ralph Connor , real name  Charles William Gordon  (1860-1937) was a Canadian author.  His works include  Black Rock: A Tale of the Selkirks  (1898),  The Sky Pilot:  A Tale of the Foothills  (1899),  The Men from Glengarry:  A Tale of the Ottawa  (1901),  Glengarry School Days:  A Story of Early Days in Glengarry  (1902),  The Foreigner:  A Tale of Saskatchewan  (1909), and  The Sky Pilot in No Man's Land  (1919).
