Robert Griffin III , known by fans and sports media as "RG3," is an American  football  player. He currently plays for the  Washington Redskins  after being selected second overall in the 2012  NFL  Draft. He played football in college for  Baylor University , and won the 2011  Heisman Trophy .

After scoring touchdowns or completing other big plays, Griffin has been known to make the  Sign of the Cross , often in combination with a kneel, a brief  prayer , and a gesture towards  Heaven . This act has been compared to  Tebowing .
