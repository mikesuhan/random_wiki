A  protist  is any of various one-celled organisms, classified in the kingdom Protista, that are either free-living or aggregated into simple colonies and that have diverse reproductive and  nutritional  modes, including the protozoans, eukaryotic algae, and slime molds. Some classification schemes also include the fungi and the more primitive bacteria and blue-green algae or may distribute the  organisms  between the kingdoms Plantae and  Animalia  according to dominant characteristics.
