Stromness  is a small port town on the island of Mainland,  Orkney . It stands at the western entrance to  Scapa Flow , and is the main port connecting Orkney with the mainland of  Scotland , with services to Scrabster, near  Thurso , and to  Aberdeen . Stromness Museum has a collection relating to the  First World War   German  fleet that was scuttled in Scapa Flow in 1919.
