Linda Lingle  was the sixth  Governor  of  Hawaii . A  Republican , she was first elected in 2002 and re-elected in 2006. She made history as the first female governor of the Aloha State.

In October 2011, Lingle announced her candidacy for the seat of retiring Senator  Daniel Akaka . She was defeated by Democratic candidate  Mazie Hironi  in the 2012 election.
