John Walton  (b. 1738; d. 1783) was a  Georgia  delegate to the  Continental Congress  in 1778. [1]

Born in Virginia in 1738, Walton became a planter near Augusta, Georgia.  He was elected to the Provincial Congress in 1775.

Walton was elected to the  Continental Congress  in 1778.  He signed the  Articles of Confederation  on July 24, 1778.

John Walton died at New Savannah, Georgia, in 1783.
