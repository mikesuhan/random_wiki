Philip Cooney  was chief of staff of the  White House   Council on Environmental Quality  during the  George W. Bush  administration.  In 2005, he left that job to work for  Exxon Mobil .  Previously, he had worked as a  lobbyist  for the  oil industry .   He has recently come under malicious and  deceitful  attack from the  environmentalism  lobby for exercising executive control over government climate reports to emphasize the uncertainty in the scientific community about  Global warming .
