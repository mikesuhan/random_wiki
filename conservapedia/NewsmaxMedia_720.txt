Newsmax Media  is an organization which publishes news from a neutral to conservative viewpoint. They publish a monthly  magazine , maintain a website, and offer a cable  television  channel, NewsMax TV. They also send out daily emails of breaking news stories of interest that may not be fully covered in the general media. Newsmax was founded to point out  liberal bias  and improprieties in the media that are forced upon the American people, and they report true news.  They also seek to extol men and women who show  conservative  virtues.
