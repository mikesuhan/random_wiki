Batman v. Superman: Dawn of Justice  is a 2016  superhero  film and the sequel to 2013's  Man of Steel . Being the second installment of Warner Bros.' planned  DC Cinematic Universe  (the  DC Comics  variant of Disney's  Marvel Cinematic Universe ), the film is intended to serve as a precursor to the up-coming two part  Justice League  film adaptation, due for release in 2018 and 2019, respectively.
