Canidae  (or  canines ) are foxes, dogs, and wolves, a  family  of  mammals  in the suborder  Caniformia  of the order  Carnivora . Members of the Canidae family are called  canids . 
The Canidae family is divided into the tribes Canini and Vulpini, Canini being the "true dogs" (or canines), and Vulpini being the "true foxes".
