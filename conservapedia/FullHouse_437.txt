Full House  is an American situation comedy that aired on  ABC , which promotes  family values , character development and many  morals . The show is about Danny Tanner, a widower father of three daughters after his wife was killed by a drunk driver. His brother in law, Jesse Katsopolis and best friend Joey Gladstone, move in with them to help raise the girls. Later in the series, Jesse gets married to Rebecca Donaldson, who not only becomes the girl's aunt but becomes a mother-figure for the girls.

Starting in 2016 a sequel titled  Fuller House  debuted, and unfortunately promotes more liberal values, but nevertheless is still pro-family.
