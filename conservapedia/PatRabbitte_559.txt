Pat Rabbitte  is a member of  Dail Eireann  for the  Irish Labour Party .  He was born in  County Mayo  in 1949.  After attending university he became a Trade Union official and also became involved in the  Marxist   Worker's Party of Ireland .  In 1989 he was elected to Dail Eireann for the party.  Other party members included  Eoghan Harris  and  Eamon Gilmore .

IN 1992, Rabbitte and five other TDs left the Worker's Party to found the Democratic Left Party.  This party merged with the Irish Labour Party in 1999.  Rabbitte became leader of the merged party in 2002 and held the position until August, 2007. [1]
