The  Philadelphia Flyers  are a  hockey  team based in  Philadelphia ,  Pennsylvania . They are members of the Atlantic Division of the Eastern Conference of the National Hockey League ( NHL ). They were founded in the 1967 Expansion, and would win the Stanley Cup in 1973-74 and again in 1974-75. The Flyers have won the Eastern Division  Title 5 additional times, but have not won the Stanley Cup since 1975.  The Flyers play in the Wachovia Center, which is also the home of the  Philadelphia 76ers .
