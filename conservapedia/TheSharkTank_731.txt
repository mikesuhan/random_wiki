The Shark Tank  "Welcome to  Florida 's Political Feeding Frenzy," is a  conservative media  website covering politics in swing-state Florida. The site is busy following the antics of  Debbie Wasserman Schultz  and former Republican  Charlie Crist  and liberal  Alan Grayson . In addition, the rising stars of the  GOP   Marco Rubio  and  Allen West  are well documented. More stories can be found about  Ann Coulter  and  Rush Limbaugh , both of which call Florida home.

Their site has been nationally recognized for it's unique stories and professional videos. The Shark Tank won the  Best News Blog  award at  CPAC  2011.
