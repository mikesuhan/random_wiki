Steven Levitt  is a  liberal  economist and co-author of  Freakonomics , a book falsely claiming legalized  abortion  contributed to a drop in the crime rate in the 1990s (a phenomenon accurately attributed to  conservative  social policies). Levitt's theory has been compared to  Eugenics . [Who says?]   He supports the privatization of prison labor and online gambling, opposes teachers' unions, and denies climate change. [1]
