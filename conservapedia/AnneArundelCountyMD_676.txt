Anne Arundel County  is a  county  in central  Maryland  that is bordered by  Baltimore  and  Baltimore County  to the north, the  Chesapeake Bay  to the east,  Howard County  to the northwest,  Prince George's County  to the west, and  Calvert County  to the south. [1] [2]

Anne Arundel County was established during the April session of the Acts of 1650 and was named after the Lady Anne Arundell.
