The Dark Side of the Moon  is a 1973 album by the British rock band  Pink Floyd . It is one of the five best selling albums of all time, having sold over 45 million copies worldwide. [1]  It also spawned the classic rock radio staples  Time ,  Money , and  Brain Damage . The album can be synched with  The Wizard of Oz  in a manner that brings about many coincidences, [2]  although various members of the band have stated that this itself is a coincidence. [3]
