George Alan O'Dowd  is a  British  pop  singer  and  DJ  better known by his stage name  Boy George , during the 1980s lead singer of the pop/new wave music group  Culture Club . The former  heroin  addict was convicted of  cocaine  possession in  Manhattan  in 2006. He was convicted for handcuffing a male escort to a bed against his will and then beating him with a chain in 2008. [1]  He was sentenced to fifteen months' imprisonment and served four months.
