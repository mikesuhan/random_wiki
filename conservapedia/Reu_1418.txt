Reu  ( Hebrew :  רְעוּ ,  Re'u ; "his friend, his shepherd") (1787 AM-2026 AM or 2217 BC-1978 BC) is the named son of  Peleg . He was born in 1787 AM. When he was 32 years old, he had a son named  Serug . He lived for another 207 years and had other sons and daughters. [1]  Altogether his life span was 239 years.
