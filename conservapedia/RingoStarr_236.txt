Ringo Starr , born Richard Starkey in 1940, was the drummer for the English rock music group,  The Beatles . Unlike the other members, Starr seldom wrote songs for the band. However, he wrote a plethora of songs for his solo career with a self-created band, Ringo Starr and His All Star Band. Following his stint with the Beatles, Starr took up acting, notably in the earlier episodes of the series, " Thomas the Tank Engine ". He was the oldest member of the Beatles, and as of 2010, he was 70 years old.
