Richard Herrnstein  (1930 - 1994) co-authored the book  The Bell Curve , published in 1994, with  Charles Murray .

Herrnstein was born May 20, 1930 was was a professor of psychology at Harvard University. [1]   His major discovery was the matching law, how animals relate their choices with rewards. [2]   He worked with  B.F. Skinner . [3]   He died September 13, 1994, of lung cancer. [4]
