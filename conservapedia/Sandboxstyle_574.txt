The term "sandbox style" gameplay refers to  Grand Theft Auto 's style of open world, unrestricted roaming.  Grand Theft Auto III , released in 2001, is credited with defining the genre. In sandbox style games, such as the Grand Theft Auto series and Grand Theft Auto clone  Saints Row , players are free to roam through the city unimpeded by physical or virtual barriers.
