He helped to translate many works of  Aristotle  into  Arabic .  His own works include  On First Philosophy  and  On the Intellect .  He developed theories regarding the soul, the universe, and taxonomy of human intellect and the psyche.  He considered Aristotle to be the authoritative source on such subjects.  He also drew on the  Isagoge  to attack  Christian  doctrine and the Trinity.
