Elijah the Prophet  translates into Hebrew as 'Eliyahu Ha-Navi'. The  Jewish  concept of  Elijah  is based on the  Bible  and on the commentaries attributed to Rashi, and Shimon Bar-Yochai.

Elijah  is a  Tishbite , and a  Prophet . Read Ezekiel to learn more about the  Chariot .
His student  Elisha  is on record as a performer of miracles, and there are twice as many recorded miracles associated with Elisha than with Elijah.  Jesus  in the  four   Gospels  is on record performing 32 miracles.
