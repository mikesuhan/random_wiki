Human Events  was founded as a  conservative   magazine  in 1944.  Its name comes from the opening of the  Declaration of Independence : "When in the course of human events ..."

Eagle Publishing  purchased  Human Events  and also a conservative book publisher, Regnery Publishing, Inc.  Its current editors are Thomas S. Winter and Terence P. Jeffrey, and its prominent  columnists  include  Robert Novak  and  Ann Coulter .

President  Ronald Reagan  would describe  Human Events  as his favorite  newspaper , [1]  saying that it "helped me stop being a liberal Democrat." [2]
