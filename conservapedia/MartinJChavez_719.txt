Martin J. Chavez  is the mayor of  Albuquerque ,  New Mexico . Chavez is a  Democrat  and was defeated in the Democratic primary in 2008 while seeking to fill the Senate seat of retiring Sen.  Pete Domenici .

Chavez served as a member of the New Mexico State Senate from 1989-93. He was elected mayor of Albuquerque  in 1993 and served till 1997. In 2001 Chavez was Democratic  candidate for Governor of New Mexico. Chavez was elected again 2001.

Chavez reported as of May 7, 2007 having raised $145,346 with cash on hand $62,341.
