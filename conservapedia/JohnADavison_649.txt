John A. Davison , born June 25, 1928, was a renowned biologist at the  University of Vermont  best known for his ground breaking scientific alternative to  Darwinism , the  prescribed evolutionary hypothesis . He has published his work extensively in peer reviewed journals and has been banned from most forums and websites about  evolution  because they did not want to debate facts [Citation Needed] .

He died of metastatic prostate cancer on April 26, 2012.
