Doug Ross  is a conservative blogger, software developer and author. Ross created the blog Doug Ross @ Journal in 2003. It is a well calculated aggregation of  conservative  news sites. In addition, Ross's blog includes insightful original content. In 2009 he expanded his offering to include "Larwyn's Linx", publishing news roundups every day. In 2012, he developed  software  robotics for his BadBlue Uncensored News Portal. This site is updated every 20 minutes and surveys thousands of websites in near real-time, 365 days a year. Categories include  guns , cars, sports, money, tech, fame in addition to political  news .

In 2013, he authored a crime novel called Hard-Boiled. It's a 99-page ebook available on Amazon. Ross describes himself as a hacker for the forces of good.
