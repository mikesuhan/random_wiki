Angela Marie "Bay" Buchanan  (born 1948) is a conservative politician and speaker.

She usually operates backstage as a campaign manager, but served as United States Treasurer (an honorific office)  under  Ronald Reagan  from March 17, 1981 to July 5, 1983, being the youngest person to hold that position at the time.

She is the sister of commentator  Pat Buchanan . She ran her brother's three campaigns for president, two inside the Republican Party and once as an independent.

Born a Roman Catholic she eventually converted to  Mormonism . She endorsed  Mitt Romney  in 2008 after directing  Tom Tancredo 's failed attempt to seek the Republican nomination.
