James Norman Mattis  (September 8, 1950 in  Pullman ) is the 26th  United States Secretary of Defense  and retired  United States Marine Corps  general, he was also responsible as commander for the USCC and the UCC for military operations in the  Middle East , Northeast Africa, and  Central Asia .

In 2016 he was nominated by then president-elect  Donald Trump  to become the new defense secretary, on January 20, 2017, he was sworn in.
