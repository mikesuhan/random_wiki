Mark V. Parkinson  (born June 24, 1957 in Wichita, Kansas) is currently the  Governor  of  Kansas  and member of the  Democratic Party . Previously a  Republican , he switched parties in 2006 to run for  Lieutenant Governor  under  Kathleen Sebelius . He assumed office as Sebelius' successor in April 2009 after being nominated by  President Obama  to serve as Secretary of Health and Human Services. Parkinson has stated he will not seek a full term in the 2010 election.
