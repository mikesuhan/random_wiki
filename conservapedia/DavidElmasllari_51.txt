David Elmasllari  (born 23 July 1992) is an  Albanian  social activist [1]  and  DJ .
He graduated with a bachelor's degree in computer science in 2014. [2]  On 17 October 2014 he became a co-founder of  Scissorkick . [3] 
He is mostly known for his  soundtrack  "Bellevue" [4]
