Aleinu  ( Hebrew :  עָלֵינוּ  "our duty") is a  Jewish   prayer  found in the  siddur , the Jewish prayerbook. It is recited at the end of each of the three daily Jewish services. It takes its name from the first line of the prayer :  Aleinu l'shabeach la'Adon hakol  - "It is our duty to praise the Master of all".
