The  Viet Minh  (from  Việt Nam Ðộc Lập Ðồng Minh Hội , "League for the Independence of Vietnam") was a  communist -led force in  Vietnam , fighting against both  French  colonial occupation and occupation by the  Japanese  during the  Second World War . Its leader was  Ho Chi Minh . The group became the government of  North Vietnam  in 1954.
