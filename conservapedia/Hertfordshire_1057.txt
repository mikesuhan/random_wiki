Hertfordshire  is a county in south-east  England , immediately north of  London . It has a population of 975,000. Its economy is largely tied to that of the capital, but the town of  Hatfield  has an aerospace industry and University.  The area also has a significant number of farmers, and has some sand and gravel quarries. Major towns include Watford, Hemel Hempstead, St Albans, Hitchin,  Letchworth ,  Welwyn Garden City  and the county town Hertford.
