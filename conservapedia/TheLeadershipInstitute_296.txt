The Leadership Institute ,  "Invest in a generation that will fight for conservative principles" , is a non-partisan, Section 501(c)(3), educational organization which identifies, recruits, trains, and places  conservatives  within the public policy process in the US and abroad. Based in Arlington,  Virginia , Morton Blackwell is the president and founded the institute in 1979. Their headquarters houses over 7,600 square feet of classroom space, state-of-the-art T.V. studios and dormitory facilities for students. The Leadership Institute is a major sponsor of the  National Tea Party Unity Convention . Also, they are involved with  Unite In Action  and their March On DC event scheduled for September 9-12th.
