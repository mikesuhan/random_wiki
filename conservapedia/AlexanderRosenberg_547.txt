Alexander Rosenberg  (born 1946) is an American philosopher,  atheist , and author. He holds the chair of the department of philosophy at  Duke University  in  North Carolina . In 2011, Rosenberg published a book entitled  The Atheist's Guide to Reality  in which he argues that atheists should not 'waste' their time debating  Christians  and other theists, but rather, should dedicate their time to learning about the universe and nature. On February 1, 2013, Rosenberg formally debated Christian philosopher and apologist  William Lane Craig  at  Purdue University  on the topic,  Is Faith in God Reasonable?

Dr. Rosenberg is also involved with the philosophy of  Economics . In 1976, his book,  Microeconomic Laws: A Philosophical Analysis  was published.
