A  Holocaust cause denier  is someone who (wrongly) denies that ideas fashionable in the 1930s among German politicians and intellectuals, such as  Joseph Mengele , had any connection with the atrocities committed in the  Holocaust .

In essence, a  Holocaust cause denier  denies that the  Holocaust  had any ideological causes.  He (wrongly) views the  Holocaust  like a random act of violence, such as murder.
