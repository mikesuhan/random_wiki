Six Flags , Inc. is the world's largest  regional  theme park company and the second leading amusement park operator in the United States,  Walt Disney  Company being the first. It maintains corporate headquarters in  New York City .  Six Flags runs 20 family-friendly theme parks in the  United States ,  Canada ,  Mexico , and  Europe . The company estimates that 65% of the U.S. population lives within 150 miles of one of its parks, located in 35 out of the top fifty major metropolitan areas.
