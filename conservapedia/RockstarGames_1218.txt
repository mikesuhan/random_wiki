Rockstar Games  is a publisher of the controversial violent  Grand Theft Auto  video game series. Rockstar Games was founded in 1998 by Sam Houser, Dan Houser, Terry Donovan, Jamie King and Gary Foreman, all of whom are  British  video game producers. Rockstar Games is headquartered in  New York City , however most of the video game production and development takes place at Rockstar North, based in  Edinburgh ,  United Kingdom . For a while, that division operated under the name "DMA Design" which developed and released  Grand Theft Auto 1  in 1997 and  Grand Theft Auto 2  in 1999.
