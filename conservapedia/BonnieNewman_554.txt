Jane Ellen "Bonnie" Newman  (born June 2, 1945) is a  New Hampshire  businesswoman and Republican.  She served as Assistant Secretary of Commerce in the Reagan administration  [1]  and as assistant for management and administration to President George H.W. Bush. 
She has a long history with the  University of New Hampshire , serving as the assistant dean of students from 1969 to 1972, dean of students from 1972-1978, and interim dean of the Whittemore School of Business and Economics from 1998-1999. [2]

On February 3, 2009, President  Barack Obama  appointed New Hampshire Senator  Judd Gregg  as  Secretary of Commerce .  In response, New Hampshire Governor  John Lynch  announced that he would appoint Newman to the  United States Senate  seat currently held by Gregg.  In appointing Newman, a  Republican , Lynch ensured that the  Democrats  would not gain a  filibuster -proof majority. [3]
