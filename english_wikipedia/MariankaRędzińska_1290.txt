Marianka Rędzińska   [maˈrjanka rɛnˈd͡ʑiɲska]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Rędziny , within  Częstochowa County ,  Silesian Voivodeship , in southern Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-east of  Częstochowa  and 70 km (43 mi) north of the regional capital  Katowice .

The village has a population of 497.
