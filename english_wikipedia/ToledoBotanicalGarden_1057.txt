Toledo Botanical Garden  is a  botanical garden  located in the city of  Toledo, Ohio . It is affiliated with the  Metroparks of the Toledo Area . Originally consisting of 20 acres (81,000 m 2 ) donated by George P. Crosby to the City of Toledo, Toledo Botanical Garden now encompasses more than 50 acres (200,000 m 2 ). Notable events include the Crosby Festival of the Arts, held in late June; and Heralding the Holidays, a seasonal celebration showcasing the numerous resident artistic guilds.
