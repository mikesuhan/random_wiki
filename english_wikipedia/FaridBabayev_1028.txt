Farid Nametalievich Babayev  ( Russian :  Фарид Наметалиевич Бабаев ; 1959 – 2007) was a Russian politician with the liberal anti-Kremlin  Yabloko  party.

Babayev was a candidate of the regional list for the party in the 2 December elections. [1]

Babayev was shot late 21 November 2007 in the entryway of his apartment building in  Makhachkala , the capital of  Dagestan . [1]  Doctors said he was shot four times, including at least once in the head. He died on 24 November of his wounds. [2]
