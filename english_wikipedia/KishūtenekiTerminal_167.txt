Kishūteneki Terminal   ( 起終点駅 ターミナル )  is a 2015 Japanese  drama film  based on the novel by Shino Sakuragi who won the  Naoki Prize  in 2013. [2] [3]  The film's theme song is "Terminal" by  My Little Lover . [4] [1]  It was released on November 7, 2015. [5]

The film grossed  ¥35.2 million  on its opening weekend. [1]
