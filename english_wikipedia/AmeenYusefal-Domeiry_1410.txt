Ameen Yusef al-Domeiry  was an alleged [ by whom? ]  member of  Egyptian Islamic Jihad . [1]

He was recruited by  Ayman al-Zawahiri  around 1979-80. [2]

A  pharmacist  by trade, he used the revenues from his business to support the group, and offered religious lessons. [1]  When  Abbud al-Zumar  hid from police, al-Domeiry found him an apartment, hid explosives for him, and gave him  EGP 800. [2]
