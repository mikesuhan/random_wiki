Balimela  is a town and a  notified area committee  in  Malkangiri district  in the  Indian   state  of  Odisha . It has several tribes and culture is odia. Odia is local language here.

Balimela is located at  18°15′N   82°08′E ﻿ / ﻿ 18.25°N 82.13°E ﻿ /  18.25; 82.13 . [1]  It has an average elevation of 418 metres (1371 feet).

As of 2001 [update]  India  census , [2]  Balimela had a population of 11,500. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Balimela has an average literacy rate of 57%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with 60% of the males and 40% of females literate. 13% of the population is under 6 years of age.
