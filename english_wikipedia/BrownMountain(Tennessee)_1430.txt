Brown Mountain  is a mountain in  Cumberland County, Tennessee , west of  Knoxville  and north of  Chattanooga . Its summit is at an elevation of 1,260 feet (380 meters) above sea level, making it the 1,095th highest mountain in the state. Nearby peaks include Buzard Hill, Wise Hill, High Point, Betsy Mountain, Johnson Mountain, and Nichols Mountain. [1]
