The Duchess's Community High School  is a  coeducational   upper school  (styled  high school ) and  sixth form  located in  Alnwick  in the  English county  of  Northumberland . [1]  It is a  community school  administered by  Northumberland County Council .

The Duchess's Community High School offers  GCSEs  and  BTEC  qualifications as programmes of study for pupils, [2]  while students in the sixth form have the option to study from a range of  A-levels  and further BTECs. [3]
