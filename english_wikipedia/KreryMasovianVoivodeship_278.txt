Krery   [ˈkrɛrɨ]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Dzierzgowo , within  Mława County ,  Masovian Voivodeship , in east-central Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of  Dzierzgowo , 19 km (12 mi) east of  Mława , and 107 km (66 mi) north of  Warsaw .
