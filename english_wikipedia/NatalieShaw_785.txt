Natalie Shaw  (born June 14, 1980) is an American actress from Topanga Canyon, California currently living in Venice, California. She played the character Shane Fusco on the television series  Under Suspicion , [1] [2]  earning her a nomination for Best Performance by a Young Actress in a Drama Series for the  Young Artist Awards , Tally in  Just One Night  with  Timothy Hutton , [3]  Rachel Kaplan on the  House MD  episode " Babies & Bathwater ", [4]  and more. She was also in a  Roman Coppola -directed Levis commercial. She is the daughter of actress  Susan Damante  and the sister of actress  Vinessa Shaw .
