Battersea Shield  is a 2004  EP  by  The Orb  and  Meat Beat Manifesto . It was sold in an embossed tin based on the design of the  Battersea Shield , instead of a standard  jewel case . [1]

A different version of "Matron" was previously released as "Horn of Jericho" on the Meat Beat Manifesto album  RUOK? . "Insane" was recorded and mixed at Concrete Hull in 2004. "1855 BC" reuses samples which were used in previous Meat Beat Manifesto tracks. [1]
