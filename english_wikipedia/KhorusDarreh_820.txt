Khorus Darreh  ( Persian :  خروس دره ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Khorūs Darreh ,  Kharūs Darreh ,  Khoroos Darreh , and  Khurus Darreh ) [1]  is a village in  Shahidabad Rural District ,  Central District ,  Avaj County ,  Qazvin Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 247, in 70 families. [2]
