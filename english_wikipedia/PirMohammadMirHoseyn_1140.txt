Pir Mohammad Mir Hoseyn  ( Persian :  پيرمحمدميرحسين ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Pīr Moḥammad Mīr Ḩoseyn ) [1]  is a village in  Kakasharaf Rural District , in the  Central District  of  Khorramabad County ,  Lorestan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. [2]
