Asmavia Iqbal Khokhar  (born 1 January 1988 [1]  in  Multan [1] ) is an international  cricketer  from  Pakistan .

Iqbal made her one day debut against  Sri Lanka  at the  National Stadium  in  Karachi , Pakistan on 28 December 2005. [1]

She was part of the team at the Women's Cricket World Cup in  Australia .and in England 2017

Iqbal was selected to play in the  2010 Asian Games  in  China . [2]
