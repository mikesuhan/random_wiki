Anastazew   [anasˈtazɛf]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Parzęczew , within  Zgierz County ,  Łódź Voivodeship , in central  Poland . [1]  It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south of  Parzęczew , 17 km (11 mi) west of  Zgierz , and 23 km (14 mi) north-west of the regional capital  Łódź .
