Jana Sedláčková  is a  Czech   football   defender , currently playing for  FF USV Jena  in the  Bundesliga . [2]  For most of her career She played for  Sparta Prague [3]  in the  Czech 1st Division  and the  Champions League . [4]

She is a member of the  Czech national team . [5]  She made her debut for the national team on 26 November 2009 in a match against  Belgium . [6]

Sedláčková was voted talent of the year at the 2009  Czech Footballer of the Year (women) . [7]
