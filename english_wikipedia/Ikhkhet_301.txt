Ikhkhet  ( Mongolian :  Иххэт ) is a  sum  (district) of  Dornogovi Province  in south-eastern  Mongolia .  Zülegt   fluorspar  mine is located in the Northern part of the sum.

Sharavyn Gungaadorj , the  Prime Minister of Mongolia  from March 21 to September 11, 1990, was born in Ikhkhet in 1935.
