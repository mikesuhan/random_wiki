Hamireh  ( Persian :  حميره ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Ḩamīreh ,  Hamīreh , and  Ḩomeyreh ; also known as  Ḩameyr  and  Humāirāh ) [1]  is a village in  Gheyzaniyeh Rural District , in the  Central District  of  Ahvaz County ,  Khuzestan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 194, in 20 families. [2]
