The  2011 UNICEF Open  was a  tennis  tournament played on  outdoor grass courts . It was the 22nd edition of the  UNICEF Open , and was part of the  250 Series  of the  2011 ATP World Tour , and of the  WTA International tournaments  of the  2011 WTA Tour . Both the men's and the women's events took place at the  Autotron park  in  Rosmalen , near  's-Hertogenbosch , Netherlands, from June 12 through June 18, 2011.
