Żabiczyn   [ʐaˈbit͡ʂɨn]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Nasielsk , within  Nowy Dwór Mazowiecki County ,  Masovian Voivodeship , in east-central Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-east of  Nasielsk , 18 km (11 mi) north-east of  Nowy Dwór Mazowiecki , and 39 km (24 mi) north of  Warsaw .
