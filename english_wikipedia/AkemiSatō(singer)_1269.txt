Akemi Satō   ( 佐藤 朱美 ,   Satō Akemi , born February 25, 1972)  is a  Japanese  female  singer  and occasional voice actress. Her body of work is entirely related to the  Fushigi Yūgi  series. [1]  She also had a small voice role (as Kaen) in the series.

All of the above songs have also appeared on various Fushigi Yūgi soundtracks.
