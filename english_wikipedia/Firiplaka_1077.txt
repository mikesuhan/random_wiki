Firiplaka  or Firiplaka Beach is a beach situated at the southern side in  Milos  Island,  Greece  between the other beaches of Provatas (West) and Tsigrado (East). It is one of the most popular beach on the island. The beach is extensive with majestic towering volcanic cliffs, snow-grey fine sand and white pebbles and shallow turquoise waters. There are umbrellas and sunbeds and a bar hut. There is also a huge rock with a cave under it in the water near the shore and it was sculptured out from a volcanic a long time ago. The access to the beach is by your own vehicle or taxi or boat which goes around the island from the port of Adamas, you just need to cross through the dirt track which it is in a good condition.
