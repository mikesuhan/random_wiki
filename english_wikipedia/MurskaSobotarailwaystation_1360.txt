Murska Sobota railway station  ( Slovene :  Železniška postaja Murska Sobota ) serves the  municipality  of  Murska Sobota ,  Slovenia . It was opened in 1924.

On 29 January 2010, the first  Holocaust  memorial in Slovenia was unveiled at the station. It is dedicated to the exile of Jews from the Prekmurje region. [1] [2]
