Bruno Postiglioni  (born  La Plata , 8 April 1987) is an Argentine rugby union player. He plays as a prop and as a hooker.

Postiglioni plays currently for  La Plata Rugby Club .

He counts 18 caps for  Argentina , since his debut at 20 May 2012, in the 40-5 win over  Uruguay , in  Santiago , for the  South American Rugby Championship . He has currently 1 try scored, 5 points on aggregate. [1]

Postiglioni was called for the  2012 Rugby Championship , but only made his debut in the competition in 2014.
