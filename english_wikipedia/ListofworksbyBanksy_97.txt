This is a  list of works by Banksy .  Banksy  is an English-based  graffiti  artist, political activist and film director whose real identity is unknown. His  satirical   street art  and subversive  epigrams  combine  dark humour  with  graffiti  executed in a distinctive  stenciling  technique. His works of  political  and  social commentary  have been featured on streets, walls, and bridges of cities throughout the world. [1]
