Kazair West  was an  airline  based in  Atyrau ,  Kazakhstan , which operated domestic corporate charter flights out of  Atyrau Airport , as well as aviation project management and medical evacuation services. [1]

The airline was established and started operations in 1996. [2]  It was registered as a shared stock company with foreign participation, represented by  Cliftondale Aviation , a  United States  company. [1]  On 13 July 2009, Kazair West was included in the  List of air carriers banned in the European Union , along with most other Kazakh airlines due to the poor maintenance standards in the country. On 18 October 2010, it had its  airline license  revoked. [3]
