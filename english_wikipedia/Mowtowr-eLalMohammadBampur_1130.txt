Mowtowr-e Lal Mohammad  ( Persian :  موتورلال محمد ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Mowtowr-e Lāl Moḩammad ) is a village in  Bampur-e Sharqi Rural District , in the  Central District  of  Bampur County ,  Sistan and Baluchestan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 135, in 29 families. [1]
