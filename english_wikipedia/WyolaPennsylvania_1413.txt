Wyola  is an  unincorporated community  in  Delaware County ,  Pennsylvania ,  United States . Wyola is located at 40°0'29"N 75°25'8"W. [1]  The location is now a traffic circle connecting St. David's Road to  Pennsylvania Route 252  near the campus of the  Episcopal Academy .

Wyola's entry in the 1894 book  Biographical and Historical Cyclopedia of Delaware County, Pennsylvania  reads in full, "Wyola has a store, postoffice, and seven houses." [2]
