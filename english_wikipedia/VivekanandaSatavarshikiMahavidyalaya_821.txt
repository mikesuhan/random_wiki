Vivekananda Satavarshiki Mahavidyalaya , also known as  Manikpara College , established in 1964, [1]  is a college in  Manikpara , in  Paschim Medinipur district . It offers undergraduate courses in arts, commerce and sciences. It is affiliated to  Vidyasagar University . [2]

The college is recognized by the  University Grants Commission  (UGC). [1]
