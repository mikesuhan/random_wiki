Islington Farmers' Market  was the first  Farmers' Market  to open in  London , it opened in 1999. The market was set up by food writer  Nina Planck , who established the organisation  London Farmers' Markets . The market is still running, having changed location twice. It is currently held on the Western end of the historic market street,  Chapel Market . [1]

In 2010, the market was voted as London's best Farmers' Market by  Time Out  magazine. [2]
