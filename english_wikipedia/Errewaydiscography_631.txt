The discography of  Argentine   pop rock  band  Erreway  consists of three  studio albums , two  compilation albums , one  live albums  and thirteen official  singles . Consisting of four leading actors from the television series  Rebelde Way  —  Camila Bordonaba ,  Felipe Colombo ,  Luisana Lopilato  and  Benjamín Rojas  — the band functioned actively from 2002 to 2005, selling approximately 10million units worldwide. [1] [2]  Several attempts of reunion were unsuccessful, and a new album  Vuelvo , recorded by Bordonaba, Colombo and Rojas, has never been released.
