Stephanie Berto  (born March 13, 1953 in  Vancouver ) is a retired female  track and field  athlete, who represented  Canada  at the  1968 Summer Olympics  in the women's  100 metres  and  4 Ã— 100 m relay , while becoming the youngest member of the olympic team at age 15. At 16, she captured a  bronze medal  in 100 meters and a  silver medal  in the 4 x 100 meter relay at the  1969 Pacific Conference Games  in  Tokyo, Japan . She won a  bronze medal  at the  1970 Commonwealth Games  in  Edinburgh, Scotland  as a member of the 4 x 100 meter relay team. She claimed the  gold medal  in the women's  200 metres  event at the  1971 Pan American Games  in  Cali, Colombia , and  silver  in the 100 metres.
