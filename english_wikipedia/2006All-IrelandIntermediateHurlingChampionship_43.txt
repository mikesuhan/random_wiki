The  2006 All-Ireland Intermediate Hurling Championship  was the 23rd staging of the All-Ireland  hurling  championship. The championship ended on 26 August 2006.

Wexford  were the  defending champions , however, they were defeated in the provincial championship.  Cork  won the title after defeating  Kilkenny  by 3-15 to 1-18 in the final. [1]
