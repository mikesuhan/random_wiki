Vigo County Home for Dependent Children , also known as the Glenn Home, is a historic  orphanage  located in  Lost Creek Township ,  Vigo County, Indiana . The main building was built in 1903, and is a 2 1/2-story,  Colonial Revival  style brick building on a raised basement. It has a hipped and gabled roof and features a semi-circular, two-story  portico  with four  Doric order  columns. Also on the property is a contributing former boiler house.
