Bieliny   [bjɛˈlinɨ]  is a  village  in  Kielce County ,  Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship , in south-central Poland. It is the seat of the  gmina  (administrative district) called  Gmina Bieliny . It lies approximately 21 km (13 mi) east of the regional capital  Kielce . [1]

The village has a population of 1,900.
