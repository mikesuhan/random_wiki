Black Stockings FC  is a Turkish "defunct" football club from  Istanbul ,  Ottoman Empire , closed in 1901.

Black Stockings FC was founded by  Fuat Hüsnü Kayacan , Mehmet Ali,  Reşat Danyal , Rıza Tevfik, Daniş, Tahsin Nahit [2]  in Istanbul in 1901. The club was dissolved after its first match on 26 October 1901, because the players were indicted after padishah's detective Ali Şamil Bey and police raided a field. [3]  The first Turkish Football player  Fuat Hüsnü Kayacan  played for Black Stockings FC. [4]
