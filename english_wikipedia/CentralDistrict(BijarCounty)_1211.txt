The  Central District of Bijar County  ( Persian :  بخش مرکزی شهرستان بیجار ‎‎) is a district ( bakhsh ) in  Bijar County ,  Kurdistan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 66,833, in 17,084 families. [1]  The District has one city:  Bijar . The District has five  rural districts  ( dehestan ):  Howmeh Rural District ,  Khvor Khvoreh Rural District ,  Najafabad Rural District ,  Seylatan Rural District , and  Siyah Mansur Rural District .
