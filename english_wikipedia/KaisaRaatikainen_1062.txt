Kaisa Raatikainen  (born 10 September 1928,  Viitasaari , died 26 February 2007,  Espoo ) was a  Finnish  politician from the  Social Democratic Party .

She was member of the parliament from 1970 to 1987, and she served as the Minister of Interior Affairs in the  Sorsa  cabinet from 1 December 1984 to 30 April 1987.  [1]

In municipality level, she was a member of  Espoo  city council from 1969 to 1992. [ citation needed ]  Her husband was the former managing director of  Yleisradio ,  Erkki Raatikainen . [ citation needed ]
