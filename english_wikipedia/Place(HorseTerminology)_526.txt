Place is a horse racing terminology and one of the popular parimutuel bet types in America, Australia, United Kingdom, Japan, Korea, Hong Kong, Macau and Singapore etc. The player has to select correctly a horse who finishes either 1st, 2nd or 3rd in a race.

How to play

Select correctly a horse who finishes either 1st, 2nd or 3rd in a race.

Dividend

e.g. If you bet 10 dollars to Place a horse and his final odds is 3.0, you will receive total 30 dollars including your stake.
