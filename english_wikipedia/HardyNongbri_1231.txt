Hardy Cliff Nongbri  is an Indian professional  footballer  who plays as a  midfielder  for  Shillong Lajong  in the  I-League .

Born in  Meghalaya , Nongbri started his footballing career with  Shillong Lajong , playing for the team in the local Shillong leagues and the  I-League U19  league. [1] [2]  Eventually, before the  2015–16 I-League , Nongbri was promoted to the first-team. [3]

He made his professional debut for the club on 10 April 2016 against  Sporting Goa . He came on as a 88th minute substitute for  Zodingliana Ralte  as Shillong Lajong lost 5–2. [4]
