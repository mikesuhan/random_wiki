Alone for the First Time  is the second studio album by Canadian record producer  Ryan Hemsworth . It was released on  Last Gang Records  and Secret Songs on November 4, 2014. [8]  Music videos were created for "Snow in Newark", [9]  "Too Long Here", [10]  and "Surrounded". [11]

At  Metacritic , which assigns a weighted average score out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics,  Alone for the First Time  received an average score of 72% based on 9 reviews, indicating "generally favorable reviews". [1]

Felicity Martin of  Clash  gave the album an 8 out of 10, saying, "It's a sad record, but one that envelops you in a warm hug, wipes your eyes and plays with your hair." [2]  Jonah Bromwich of  Pitchfork  gave the album a 6.0 out of 10, saying, " Alone for the First Time  is the furthest he's pushed himself, and the growing pains on the album can be chalked up to the strain of trying new things, a kind of adolescent awkwardness that shows signs of maturing into something sophisticated and unique." [7]

CMJ  placed it at number 3 on its list of the "30 Best Albums of 2014". [12]
