Melisa Sözen  (born 6 July 1985 in İstanbul) is a  Turkish  actress.

Sözen graduated in theatre from Pera Fine Arts College. She made her film debut in director  Çağan Irmak 's  Bana Şans Dile  and has gone on to act in several other films such as  Okul ,  O Şimdi Mahkum ,  Cenneti Beklerken ,  Eve Giden Yol 1914 ,  Para=Dolar ,  Casus Kızlar  and  Av Mevsimi .

She is best known for portraying Nihal in the  Nuri Bilge Ceylan 's  Winter Sleep , which won the  Palme d'Or  at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival.
