Manki  is an administrative unit, known as  Union council  of  Swabi District  in the  Khyber Pakhtunkhwa  province of  Pakistan .

District Swabi has 4  Tehsils  i.e.  Swabi Tehsil ,  Lahor ,  Topi Tehsil  and  Razar . Each Tehsil comprises certain numbers of union councils. There are 56 union councils in district Swabi.

Manki is one of the Beautiful village in Swabi. Manki has produce so many Doctors, Engineers and Army officers.
