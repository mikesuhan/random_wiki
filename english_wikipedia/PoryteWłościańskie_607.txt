Poryte Włościańskie   [pɔˈrɨtɛ vwɔɕˈt͡ɕaɲskʲɛ]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Stawiski , within  Kolno County ,  Podlaskie Voivodeship , in north-eastern Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of  Stawiski , 12 km (7 mi) south-east of  Kolno , and 78 km (48 mi) west of the regional capital  Białystok .
