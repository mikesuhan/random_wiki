Dingeleh Kahriz  ( Persian :  دينگله كهريز ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Dīngeleh Kahrīz  and  Dīngleh Kahrīz ; also known as  Dengeleh Kahrīz ) [1]  is a village in  Sangestan Rural District , in the  Central District  of  Hamadan County ,  Hamadan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 460, in 121 families. [2]
