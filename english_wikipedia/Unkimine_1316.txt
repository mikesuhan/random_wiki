The  Unki mine  is an underground mine located in the center part of  Zimbabwe  in  Gweru ,  Midlands Province . Unki represents one of the largest  platinum  reserves in  Zimbabwe  having estimated reserves of 34 million  oz  of  platinum . [1]  The mine produces around 64,000 oz of platinum/year. [1]
