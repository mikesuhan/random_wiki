Hizbul Islam

The  Battle of Kismayo (2009)  erupted on October 1, 2009, after the alliance ruling  Kismayo ,  Somalia  broke down. Sheikh Ahmed "Madobe" and his  Ras Kamboni Brigade  forces tried to expel  al-Shabaab  from the city but were overpowered and the battle resulted in an al-Shabaab takeover of Kismayo. [1]
