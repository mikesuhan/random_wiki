The  1982 Paris Open  was a  Grand Prix  men's  tennis  tournament played on indoor  carpet courts . It was the 14th edition of the Paris Open (later known as the  Paris Masters ). It took place at the  Palais omnisports de Paris-Bercy  in  Paris , France from 25 October through 1 November 1982.  Wojciech Fibak  won the singles title.

Wojciech Fibak  defeated    Bill Scanlon  6–2, 6–2, 6–2

Brian Gottfried  /    Bruce Manson  defeated    Jay Lapidus  /    Richard Meyer  6–4, 6–2
