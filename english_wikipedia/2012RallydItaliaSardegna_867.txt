The  9º  Rally d'Italia Sardegna  was the penultimate round of the  2012 World Rally Championship season  and was held between 18 and 21 October 2012. It was based in  Olbia , Sardegna.

The rally was also the seventh round of the  Production World Rally Championship .

The "Power stage" was a 8.24 km (5.12 mi) stage at the end of the rally. [1]
