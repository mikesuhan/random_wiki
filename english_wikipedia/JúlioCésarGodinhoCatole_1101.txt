Júlio César Godinho Catole  (ジュリーニョ | born 5 August 1986), commonly known as  Julinho , is a  Brazilian   footballer [1]  who plays for  J2 League  club  Consadole Sapporo . He primarily plays as a  left back , but he could also play as a  left wing back ,  left wide midfielder , or even as  left winger .
