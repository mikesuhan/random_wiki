Elba house  is a partnership company divided equally between the  Iraqi Ministry of Transportation  and a Jordanian family named  Khoury . The company was established in 1976 [1]  on 10 hectares of land located in  Amman ,  Jordan  with a capacity to assemble more than 2000 buses each year. The company has the capability to build all of the buses bodies using mechanical and electrical parts.
