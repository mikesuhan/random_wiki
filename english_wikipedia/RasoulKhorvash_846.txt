Rasoul Khrovash  ( Persian :  رسول خوروش ‎‎; born 30 January 1953 in  Isfahan ) is a retired  Irannian   football   midfielder . He is currently the team manager at  Sepahan . He is graduated from the  University of Texas  in sports and before this, he was graduated from  Isfahan University  in geographical. He is the brother of Mostafa Khorvash and cousin of Shokrallah Khorvash, the founder of  Sepahan .

Khorvash is one of the former players of Sepahan which he began his football career at the age of 8 in  Sepahan academy  and was promoted to the first team when he was 16. He was retired in 1984 when playing for Sepahan. His usual post was left midfielder.

He was appointed as team manager of Sepahan on 9 November 2011. [3]
