Sid Meier's Antietam!  is a  real-time tactics   computer game  designed by  Sid Meier , the co-founder of  Firaxis Games , then released in December 1999 by  Electronic Arts . It is the sequel to the 1997  Sid Meier's Gettysburg! .

The game allows the player to control either the  Confederate  or  Union  troops during the  Battle of Antietam  of the  American Civil War . It can be played as a single scenario, or as a campaign of linked scenarios, either recounting the original history or exploring alternate possibilities.

Antietam!  received "favorable" reviews according to the  review aggregation  website  Metacritic . [1]
