Shad Galdi Mahalleh  ( Persian :  شادگلدي محله ‎‎, also  Romanized  as  Shād Galdī Maḩalleh ; also known as  Shāh Galdī Maḩalleh ) [1]  is a village in  Chubar Rural District ,  Haviq District ,  Talesh County ,  Gilan Province ,  Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 141, in 32 families. [2]
