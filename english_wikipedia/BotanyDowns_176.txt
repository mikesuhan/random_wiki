Botany Downs  is a  suburb  of  Auckland ,  New Zealand . This residential area was previously part of the East Tamaki area. According to the 2006 census, Botany Downs has a population of 4830, having grown 2.7% since 2001. It has one of the country's biggest shopping malls -  Botany Town Centre . The suburb is in the Howick ward, one of the thirteen administrative divisions of Auckland City. Since  2008 , a general electorate,  Botany , carries the name of the suburb.
