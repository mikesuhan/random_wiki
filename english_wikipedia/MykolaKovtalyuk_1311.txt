Mykola Kovtalyuk  ( Ukrainian :  Микола Андрійович Ковталюк ; born 26 April 1995 in  Sambir ,  Lviv Oblast ,  Ukraine ) is a  Ukrainian   football   striker  who plays for  Dinamo Tbilisi  in the  Erovnuli Liga .  [1]

Kovtalyuk is a product of the FC Knyazha Schaslyve (his trainer was Mykola Lytovchak) and UFK-Karpaty Lviv [2]  youth sportive schools.

He played for different Ukrainian and foreign clubs, including in Slovakia and in Georgia.
