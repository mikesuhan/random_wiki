Bacze Mokre   [ˈbat͡ʂɛ ˈmɔkrɛ]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Zambrów , within  Zambrów County ,  Podlaskie Voivodeship , in north-eastern Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of  Zambrów  and 70 km (43 mi) west of the regional capital  Białystok .
