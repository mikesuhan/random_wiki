Takuma Ito   ( 伊藤 拓真 ,   Itō Takuma , born August 11, 1986)  is a  Japanese   football  player. He is currently playing for  ReinMeer Aomori  in  Japan Football League .

He has previous played in Singapore's  S.League  for  Albirex Niigata FC (Singapore)  and  Geylang International . He started his career with  Thespa Kusatsu . [1]
