Terry Crews  is an American actor and former  NFL  player. The following is a  filmography  of his work.

Crews is best known for playing  Sgt. Terry Jeffords  in the  Fox  sitcom  Brooklyn Nine-Nine ,  Julius  on the  UPN / CW  sitcom  Everybody Hates Chris  and for his appearances in  Old Spice  commercials, [1]  as well as for roles in films such as:  The Expendables  and its sequel  The Expendables 2 .

He also stars as Nick Kingston-Persons on the  TBS  sitcom  Are We There Yet?  and as himself on the  BET  reality series  The Family Crews .
