Phú Thọ  (   listen ) is an  township  in  Phú Thọ Province ,  Vietnam . As of 2003 the district had a population of 63,333. [1]  The district covers an area of 64 km². [1]

Phú Thọ township is bordered by Phu Ninh at the north, Thanh Ba at the southwest, Tam Nong at the south, Lam Thao at the southeast.
