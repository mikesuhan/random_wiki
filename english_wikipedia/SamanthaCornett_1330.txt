Samantha Cornett , (born February 4, 1991) is a professional  squash  player who represents Canada. She is from  Deep River, Ontario . She reached a career-high world ranking of World No. 28 in May 2014. [1] [2]  She won two medals at the  2011 Pan American Games , a gold in the team event alongside  Miranda Ranieri , &  Stephanie Edmison , and a silver in the singles event.
