Gaariwala / Gariwala  (English Title:  The Cart ) is a 2015  Bangladeshi   Bengali  film directed by Ashraf Shishir. The film participated in 78 international film festivals in 26 countries of 6 continents and won several international awards including NEZ International Film Festival in  Kolkata , India. [1]  At Texas film festival the film won seven awards. [2] [3]

The film released on 4 November 2015 and mostly received positive reviews. [3] [4]
