Robert Norval Garrett  (February 15, 1904 – April 20, 1973) was an  American football  coach. He was the first head football coach at Southeastern Louisiana College—now known as  Southeastern Louisiana University —in  Hammond, Louisiana  and he held that position for the 1930 season. His coaching record at Southeastern Louisiana was 2–4. [1]  He died at a  Hammond, Louisiana  hospital in 1973. [2]
