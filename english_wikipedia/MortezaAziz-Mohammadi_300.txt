Morteza Aziz-Mohammadi  (born 8 August 1985) [1]  is an  Iranian   footballer  who currently plays for  Giti Pasand  in  Azadegan League .

In 2009, Aziz-Mohammadi joined  Pas Hamedan F.C.  after spending the previous 3 seasons at  Paykan F.C. . [2]  On 14 July 2011, He was joined to the  Shahin Bushehr .
