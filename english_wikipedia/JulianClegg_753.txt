Julian Clegg  (born in  Hampshire ,  England ) is a presenter [1]  on  LIVING  TV's  paranormal television  show;  Most Haunted Live! . He is the face of the interactive part of the show. Julian also presents the Breakfast Show on  BBC Radio Solent  weekdays from 6:30 - 10am.

His first break was with  BBC Local Radio  in  Sussex , but moved back to his boyhood home in the  New Forest . It is near  New Milton .
