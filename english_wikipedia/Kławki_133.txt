Kławki   [ˈkwafki]  ( German :  Klakendorf ) is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Stare Pole , within  Malbork County ,  Pomeranian Voivodeship , in northern Poland. [1]  It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of  Stare Pole , 13 km (8 mi) east of  Malbork , and 53 km (33 mi) south-east of the regional capital  Gdańsk .

Before 1945 the area was part of  Germany . For the history of the region, see  History of Pomerania .

The village has a population of 126.
