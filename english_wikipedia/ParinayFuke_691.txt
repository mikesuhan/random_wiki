Parinay Fuke  (born 5 January 1981) is a member of  Maharashtra Legislative Council , belonging to the  Bharatiya Janata Party  elected as Member of Legislative Council from Bhandara-Gondia Constituency. [1]

Fuke attended Nagpur University for his Ph.D.

Fuke won Nagpur Municipal Corporation Corporation Election independently in 2007 with massive margin votes of 4000.
