Ivan Yevgenyevich Knyazev  ( Russian :  Иван Евгеньевич Князев ; born 5 November 1992 in  Timashyovsk ) is a  Russian   football   defender . He plays for  FC Ural Yekaterinburg .

Knyazev made his debut in the  Russian National Football League  for  Torpedo Moscow  on 12 March 2013 in a game against  Yenisey Krasnoyarsk . [1]
