The  2011  Kazan Kremlin Cup  was a professional  tennis  tournament played on  indoor hard courts . It was the second edition of the tournament which was part of the  2011 ATP Challenger Tour  and the  Tretorn SERIE+  series. [1]  It took place in  Kazan ,  Russia  between 31 January and 6 February 2011.
