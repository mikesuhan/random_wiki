Farariana  is a town in  Madagascar . It is a part of the commune of  Nandihizana  in the District of  Manjakandriana , and is located 35Â km from the city of  Antananarivo . Farariana was established in the 17th century and can count among its rulers one of the sisters of  Andrianampoinimerina , the king who reunited the Kingdom of  Imerina  at the turn of the 19th century. At the time of its founding, approximately 1000 people lived in Farariana. After  World War II , numerous businessmen born in Farariana moved to Antananarivo and started lumber and woodworking businesses.
