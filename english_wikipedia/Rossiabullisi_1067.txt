Rossia bullisi , also known as the  Gulf bobtail squid , is a species of  bobtail squid  native to the tropical western  Atlantic Ocean , specifically the northern  Gulf of Mexico  and  Straits of Florida . [1]

R. bullisi  grows to 45 mm in  mantle  length. [1]

The  type specimen  was collected in the  Gulf of Mexico  and is deposited at the  National Museum of Natural History  in  Washington, D.C. . [2]
