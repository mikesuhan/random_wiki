Quaker Springs  is a  hamlet  in  Saratoga County . The  zip code  is 12871. Quaker Springs is between NY-32 Route 4. Quaker Springs United Methodist Church is located in the hamlet. [1]  An anti-slavery society was based in Quaker Springs and several possible participants in the  Underground Railroad  were in Quaker Springs. [2]  The name Quaker Springs came from a type of mineral spring located in the area. [3]  The area including Quaker Springs was involved in Revolutionary War positions. [4]
