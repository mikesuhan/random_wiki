Monett  is an  unincorporated community  in  Sedan Township, Chautauqua County, Kansas , in the  United States . [1]

A post office was first opened in Mount Vernon (an extinct town), but it was moved to Monett in 1887 and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1918. [2]
