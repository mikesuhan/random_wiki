Cécile Storti  (born April 5, 1983 in  Évian-les-Bains ) is a  French   cross-country skier  who has competed since 2000. Competing in two  Winter Olympics , she earned her best finish of sixth in the 4 x 5 km relay at  Vancouver  in  2010 .

Storti's best finish at the  FIS Nordic World Ski Championships  was ninth in the 4 x 5 km relay at  Oberstdorf  in  2005 .

Her best World Cup finish was fifth at a 4 x 5 km relay in  Italy  in 2006 while her best individual finish was 19th at a 7.5 km + 7.5 km double pursuit event in  Canada  in 2009.
