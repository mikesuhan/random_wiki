Qminas  ( Arabic :  قميناس ‎‎ also spelled  Qaminas  or  Kominas ) is a village in northwestern  Syria , administratively part of the  Idlib Governorate , located 6 kilometers (3.7 mi) southeast of  Idlib . Nearby localities include  Sarmin  to the northeast and  Saraqib  to the east. [1]  According to the  Syria Central Bureau of Statistics , Qminas had a population of 2,722 in the 2004 census. [2]
