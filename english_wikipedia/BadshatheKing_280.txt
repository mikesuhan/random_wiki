Badsha the King  is a 2004  Bengali film  directed by Saurav Chakraborty and produced by Suman Kumar Das under the banner of Burima Chitram. The film features actors  Prosenjit Chatterjee  and  Koel Mallick  in the lead roles. The film was scored by  Ashok Bhadra  and Snehasish Chakraborty. [1]

Ashok Bhadra  and Snehasish Chakraborty composed the film score of  Badsha The King .
