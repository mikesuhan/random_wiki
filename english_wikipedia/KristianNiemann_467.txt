Kristian Niemann  is a guitarist and is or has been in bands such as  Therion , [1]   Lithium  and  Demonoid . He studied  jazz  at  Musicians Institute . His style of playing mostly centers on  legato ,  sweep picking , and he sometimes uses two-handed  tapping  to play extremely fast shreds. In 1996 his song "Industrial Type Thang" was featured in  JSR Records ' compilation  Hottest Unknown Guitarists . He is also brother of  Johan Niemann .
