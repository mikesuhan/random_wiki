Estadio Arquitecto Ricardo Etcheverri  is a  football  stadium located in the  Caballito  district of  Buenos Aires . It is the home venue of  Ferro Carril Oeste . The stadium was opened in 1905, and has a current capacity of 24,442. [1]

It is located close to Caballito station on the  Ferrocarril Sarmiento  line. [2]
