Nadiya Mykolaïvna Myronyuk  ( Ukrainian :  Надія Миколаївна Миронюк ; born March 25, 1984 in  Volyn Oblast ) is a  Ukrainian   weightlifter . [1]

Myronyuk represented  Ukraine  at the  2008 Summer Olympics  in  Beijing , where she competed for the  women's heavyweight category  (75 kg). Myronyuk placed ninth in this event, as she successfully lifted 105 kg in the single-motion  snatch , and hoisted 132 kg in the two-part, shoulder-to-overhead  clean and jerk , for a total of 237 kg.
