Kees Akerboom  is a Dutch professional basketball player currently with the  DBL  team  New Heroes Den Bosch . Kees is the son of  Kees Akerboom Sr. , a former successful professional basketball player as well. In his career, Akerboom played for the  Netherlands national basketball team .

Akerboom started his professional career with the same team as his father did,  EiffelTowers Den Bosch  in the  Dutch Basketball League . He played three seasons in  Den Bosch  before he left for the  MPC Capitals  from  Groningen . Akerboom stayed two years in Groningen before he returned to his old team EiffelTowers. He won his first Dutch championship in 2007, his second in 2012.
