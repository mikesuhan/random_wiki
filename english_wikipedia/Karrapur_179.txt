Karrapur  is a  census town  in  Sagar district  in the  Indian   state  of  Madhya Pradesh .

Karrapur is located at  23°57′N   78°51′E ﻿ / ﻿ 23.95°N 78.85°E ﻿ /  23.95; 78.85 . [1]  It has an average elevation of 497 metres (1630 feet).

As of 2001 [update]  India  census , [2]  Karrapur had a population of 9285. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Karrapur has an average literacy rate of 61%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 72%, and female literacy is 48%. In Karrapur, 18% of the population is under 6 years of age.
