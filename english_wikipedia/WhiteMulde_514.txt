The  White Mulde  ( German :  Weiße Mulde ) is the right  headstream  of the  Zwickau Mulde  in the  Vogtland  region of  Saxony ,  Germany .

The White Mulde rises north of Kottenheide, a village in the borough of  Schöneck , at an elevation of  770 m above NN . Its source is in the pond of  Oberer Muldeteich . The White Mulde runs in a northerly direction, through the  Unterer Muldeteich  and discharges after about 3 km into the  Muldenberg Reservoir . There it unites with the  Red Mulde  to form the  Zwickau Mulde .
