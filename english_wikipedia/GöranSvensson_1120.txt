Göran Svensson  (8 March 1959 – 6 October 1995) was a Swedish  discus thrower . He represented Sweden at the  1987 World Championships in Athletics . As a senior, he competed for Söderhamns IF, IF Göta and IF Elfsborg . He won national championships in 1982 and also took a total of four SM-silver. In 1985 he was suspended 18 months for doping. While competing for  Brigham Young University  he was the 1980  NCAA Champion . After studies in the U.S., he settled there and became a U.S. citizen in 1988. However, he is buried on Norrala Cemetery in Söderhamn. [1]
