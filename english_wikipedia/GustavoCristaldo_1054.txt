Gustavo Cristaldo  (born 31 May 1989) is a  Paraguayan  international  footballer  who plays for  Universidad de Concepción , as a  midfielder .

Cristaldo has played for  Libertad ,  Sol de América  and  Rubio Ñu . [1]

He made his international debut for  Paraguay  in 2011, [1]  and participated at the  2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup . [2]
