Stephen Martin Edge  (born 29 November 1950 [1] ) is one of the  UK 's foremost  corporate tax   lawyers . He was born and raised in  Bolton ,  Lancashire . After schooling at  Canon Slade School [2]  he went to  Exeter University  where he achieved a 2:2 in law. In 1973 he joined  Slaughter and May  where he has been a partner since 1982. [3]

He is now one of the UK's leading authorities on corporate tax law. [4]

He is vice-president of the Lancashire Cricket Federation and a member of the Marylebone Cricket Club.
