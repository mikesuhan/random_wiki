Langøy  or  Langøyneset  is a village in  Averøy Municipality  in  Møre og Romsdal  county,  Norway . It is located on the northern part of the island of  Averøya . It is located mainly on the small island of Langøya, off the northern coast of the main island of  Averøya . The village is west of the village of  Bremsnes  and northeast of the village of  Kårvåg . The  Langøy Chapel  is located here. The 0.65-square-kilometre (160-acre) village has a population (2012) of 359, which gives the village a  population density  of 552 inhabitants per square kilometre (1,430/sq mi). [1]  The  Hestskjær Lighthouse  lies about 2 kilometres (1.2 mi) north of this village.
