Ashes to Ashes  is the third album by the  deathcore  band  Chelsea Grin  released on July 8, 2014 via  Artery  and Razor and Tie. [7]

AllMusic  described the sound of the album as  deathcore , and on some songs  metalcore  relating it to that of their previous two studio albums. [5]

Ashes to Ashes  was released on July 8, 2014 by  Artery Recordings . [5]

Allmusic gave the album three and a half stars out of five, noting that "Chelsea Grin marry their punishing breakdowns with some pretty dazzling guitar work, showing that there's more to their sound than just smashing the listener into oblivion." [5]
