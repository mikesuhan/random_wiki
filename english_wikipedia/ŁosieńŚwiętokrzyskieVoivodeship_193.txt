Łosień   [ˈwɔɕeɲ]  is a  village  in the administrative district of  Gmina Piekoszów , within  Kielce County ,  Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship , in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) west of  Piekoszów  and 18 km (11 mi) west of the regional capital  Kielce . [1]

The village has a population of 270.
